ANNOUNCEMENT CONFIRMATION UPDATE

- Super Admin can send important announcements to agents, admins, or everyone.
- Acknowledgement can be required; the popup cannot be closed until confirmed.
- Each confirmation is written to Firebase with name, role, and timestamp.
- Super Admin has a live Sent / Confirmed / Pending report.
- Recipient filters show who acknowledged and who is still pending.
- Announcement history preserves prior reports and confirmation progress.
- Active announcements can be closed without deleting their history.
