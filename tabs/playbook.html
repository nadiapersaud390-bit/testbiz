💰 WHAT YOU'RE ASKING FOR
You need their 2025 gross annual revenue — that's the total the business brought in before any taxes, deductions, or expenses. Not what they kept. The full top-line number.
🎯 STEP A
"Does your business generate at least $1 million a year in revenue?"
✅ YES
"Can you please provide a rough estimate of how much?"
QUALIFIED — Transfer (Over $1M Line)
➡️ NO
No problem — continue to Step B below.
MOVE TO NEXT QUESTION
🎯 STEP B
"Does your business generate at least $200K in revenue per year?"
✅ YES
"Can you please provide a rough estimate of how much?"
QUALIFIED — Transfer (Under $1M Line)
❌ NO
Revenue is below minimum. Does not qualify.
DNO — END CALL
📝 IF THEY'RE UNSURE OF THEIR ANNUAL NUMBER
"No problem — roughly how much does the business bring in each month?"
Multiply their monthly number by 12 to get an annual estimate.
⚠️ IF THEY SAY "IT SHOULD BE AROUND THE SAME AS LAST YEAR"
If you're still unsure about their numbers, it might be worth asking them to provide more details.
⚠️ IF QUALIFIERS ARE NOT MET YOU CANNOT TRANSFER!!!

KNOW BEFORE YOU TRANSFER

INDUSTRY DRILL-DOWN

TRACTOR
DROPOUT ON TRACTOR TYPE
ASK FORT ▼
🔑 KEY QUESTION TO ASK
"Is it a detachable tractor?"
❌ DETACHABLE TRACTOR = TRUCK — CANNOT WORK
A detachable tractor is classified as a truck. This falls under the No Trucking rule — end and move on.
✅ NON-DETACHABLE (FARM / EQUIPMENT) — CAN WORK
Farm tractors or heavy equipment that are not detachable semis can qualify.
ASK FIRST
LOGGING
MUST OPERATE ALL 12 MONTHS — NO EXCEPTIONS
ASK FORT ▼
📋 WHY THIS MATTERS
Logging is a seasonal industry. Many operators shut down in winter. To qualify, they must be operating all 12 months of the year.
🎯 STEP 1
"Is this a logging or timber business?"
🎯 STEP 2 — THE CRITICAL QUESTION
"How many months out of the year is your business actively operating?"
🔴 UNDER 12 MONTHS — CANNOT WORK
Any seasonal shutdown disqualifies them.
🟢 FULL 12 MONTHS — CAN WORK
Year-round confirmed — proceed to transfer.
CONSTRUCTION
DROPOUT ON PUNO USE
DEAL COINS ▼
🎯 STEP 1
"Does your construction business deal with trucking?"
🎯 STEP 2 — IF YES – TRUCKING INVOLVED
"Is trucking a major part of your business?"
🎯 STEP 3 — IF YES – MAJOR PART
"What will you be using the funds for?"
❌ FUNDS FOR TRUCKS — CANNOT WORK
If funds are going toward trucks or trucking equipment — cannot proceed.
✅ FUNDS FOR CREW / PAYROLL / OTHER — CAN WORK
If funds are for crew, payroll, materials, or anything besides trucking — transfer.
✅ NO TRUCKING INVOLVED — CAN WORK
Construction with no trucking component qualifies. Proceed with transfer.

"What is your profession or primary occupation?"
⚖️ LAWYER OR ATTORNEY — CANNOT WORK. PERIOD.

TRANSPORTATION
DROPOUT ON TRANSPORT TIPS
ASK FORT ▼
🔑 KEY QUESTION TO ASK
"What type of transportation does your business operate?"
❌ DETACHABLE TRACTOR (SEMI / 18-WHEELER)
CANNOT WORK — Classified as trucking.
❌ TRUCK
CANNOT WORK — Trucking business.
✅ VAN
CAN WORK — Vans are acceptable. Ask what they use it for.
❌ FREIGHT CARRIER
CANNOT WORK — Freight/cargo restrictions apply.
✅ BUS / COACH / SHUTTLE / TAXI / RIDESHARE
CAN WORK — Passenger transport is acceptable.
📌 BOTTOM LINE
Detachable tractor, truck, or freight carrier = NO TRANSFER. Vans = OK with confirmation.
